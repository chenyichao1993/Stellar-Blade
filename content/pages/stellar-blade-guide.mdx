## Core Combat Tips to Start

Stellar Blade's combat rewards patience and timing over button mashing. Here are the essentials every player should know:

- **Perfect Parry** is your best defensive tool. It negates damage, builds Beta Energy, and reduces enemy balance. Invest in Focus Boost early if you struggle with timing.
- **Perfect Dodge** (Blink) handles yellow-glow unblockable attacks and teleports you behind the enemy for a counter.
- **Beta Skills** are powerful abilities fueled by Beta Energy. Triplet and Shield Breaker are the strongest early picks.
- **Burst Skills** consume a separate meter. Tempest and Descending Break are excellent for crowds and bosses respectively.
- **Tachy Mode** unlocks later and turns EVE invulnerable for a short time while massively boosting damage.

Spend the first few hours practicing parry timing on basic Naytibas. The muscle memory you build early will carry you through the hardest boss fights.

## How Endings Work

Stellar Blade has three endings, and two of them depend on a hidden metric: **Lily's progress bar**. This bar fills as you collect documents, Memory Sticks, hidden cans, and complete certain side quests.

- **Return to the Colony** — Refuse Adam's hand at the end. This is the "good" ending.
- **Cost of Lost Memories** — Accept Adam's hand with Lily's progress below 100%.
- **Making New Memories** — Accept Adam's hand with Lily's progress at 100%. This also unlocks the secret Eidos 9 stage.

We recommend a thorough first playthrough to reach 100% Lily progress, then using New Game Plus for the remaining ending. See our [all endings guide](/all-endings-guide/) for the full strategy.

## Bosses Worth Preparing For

Some Naytiba bosses are significantly harder than the rest. Make sure you upgrade your weapon, stock healing items, and equip the right Exospine before these fights:

- **Raven (Naytiba Form)** — Fast, airborne, and capable of an instant-kill charge attack.
- **Tachy** — A multi-phase duel with a black-wing transformation and tentacle attacks.
- **Behemoth** — Brutal speed and power combo; requires careful stamina management.
- **Karakuri** — Disables your drone, forcing pure melee and grenade play.
- **Maelstrom** — Three weak spots plus adds; mobility is essential.

Each boss has a dedicated strategy page with phase breakdowns and recommended skills.

## Collectibles and Secrets

Stellar Blade hides dozens of optional rewards across its world. The most sought-after collectibles include:

- **49 beverage cans** — scattered through every region; collecting all unlocks the Black Pearl outfit.
- **25 fish species** — catch them all for the Ocean Maid outfit and the Lonely Fisherman achievement.
- **Passcodes** — open locked doors and chests in Eidos 7, Wasteland, Matrix 11, and beyond.
- **Nano Suits** — over 40 cosmetic outfits to discover, purchase, or craft.
