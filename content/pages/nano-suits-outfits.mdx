## What Are Nano Suits?

Nano Suits are cosmetic outfits that change Eve’s appearance without altering her stats or combat abilities. They are one of the main rewards for exploring Stellar Blade, and collecting them all is a long-term goal for many players. You can swap suits at any Repair Console, letting you dress Eve for the situation or simply show off your rarest looks.

Because Nano Suits are purely visual, you never need to worry about choosing the "wrong" outfit for combat. The real challenge is finding the materials and meeting the unlock conditions for each design.

## How Crafting Works at the Repair Console

Most Nano Suits are crafted at a Repair Console using materials gathered from the world. The required ingredients vary by suit. Common materials include enemy drops, ore, plants, and fish. Rare suits often need components from late-game areas or New Game Plus.

To craft a suit, approach a Repair Console, open the Nano Suit menu, and select the design you want. If you have the materials, the suit is added to your wardrobe permanently. Any suit you craft in one playthrough carries over to [New Game Plus](/new-game-plus-guide/), so your collection stays intact across runs.

## How to Get Materials

Materials come from several sources:

- **Enemy drops** — Naytibas and wildlife drop scales, claws, and other components.
- **Resource nodes** — Ore and plants can be harvested from glowing points in each region.
- **Fishing** — Several suits require fish or fishing-related materials. Our [Fishing Guide](/fishing-guide/) covers every catch and bait type.
- **Chests and locked rooms** — Passcode doors and hidden chests sometimes contain rare materials. Our [All Passcodes & Codes guide](/all-passcodes-codes/) lists every code.
- **NG+ exclusive sources** — New Game Plus adds materials and currencies needed for the expanded Nano Suit catalog.

## Notable Nano Suits

### Skin Suit

The Skin Suit is one of the earliest and most talked-about outfits. It replaces Eve’s standard armor with a sleek, form-fitting design. It is unlocked through normal progression and serves as a good example of how Nano Suits change Eve’s silhouette without affecting gameplay.

### Black Pearl

The Black Pearl outfit is the reward for collecting all 49 beverage cans scattered across the world. It is one of the most time-consuming suits to earn because it requires full exploration of nearly every region. If you are working toward the true ending, the can hunt overlaps nicely with raising Lily’s progress. See the [Collectibles Guide](/collectibles-guide/) for tracking help.

### Ocean Maid

Ocean Maid is the fishing reward. To unlock it, you must catch all 25 fish in the game. That means unlocking every fishing spot, acquiring every bait type, and completing the fishing quest chain. The [Fishing Guide](/fishing-guide/) breaks down every species and location.

### Raven Suit

The Raven Suit is one of the standout additions in New Game Plus. Its darker design and detailed textures make it a favorite among players who complete a second run. NG+ adds around 30 such exclusive outfits, so collectors will want to jump into a second playthrough.

### Planet Diving Variants

Planet Diving suits come in several color variants. These are tied to different progression milestones and crafting recipes. Collecting every variant is a good long-term goal once you have finished the main story.

## Tips for Collectors

- **Explore thoroughly** — Many suits require materials from hidden paths and locked rooms.
- **Track your progress** — The in-game menus show which Nano Suits you own and which are still locked.
- **Plan for NG+** — If a suit requires late-game or NG+ materials, do not waste early resources trying to craft it prematurely.
- **Check passcodes** — Several locked areas contain materials or outfit-related items. Keep the [All Passcodes & Codes guide](/all-passcodes-codes/) handy.
- **Fish early and often** — Fishing is the only source for some materials, so unlock it as soon as you reach the Great Desert Oasis.

With patience and a full completion plan, you can fill Eve’s wardrobe with every Nano Suit Stellar Blade has to offer.
